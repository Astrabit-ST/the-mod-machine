Pop-up Hints

RPGXP contains simple explanations for the controls in almost every dialog box so you can get the information you need without disrupting your workflow. These are called "pop-up hints." To display them, use one of the following methods.

  1. Press F1 while using a control.
  2. Right-click the control to bring up the pop-up menu, then select [What's This?] (see right). This method may not be available for all control types.
  3. Click the [?] icon on the right edge of the titlebar, then left-click the control in question.
